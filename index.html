Boot Camp

From zero to fluent

No prior knowledge required. 14 structured lessons teach you every letter and number through hands-on drills with a realistic telegraph key. Each lesson builds on the last — by graduation, you'll be decoding full sentences in real time.

  • LEARN — See and hear each character's dot-dash pattern
  • DRILL — Practice sending characters on the telegraph key with instant feedback
  • FIELD EXERCISE — Decode full words using everything you've learned
  • FINAL EXAM — A 3-phase cumulative test to earn your graduation

4 Mission Types

Every mode, a different challenge

Graduate Boot Camp and your real deployment begins. Four distinct game modes test speed, accuracy, and nerve — each inspired by actual wartime radio operations.

Radio Operator

Transmit

Decode authentic WWII dispatches letter-by-letter using a telegraph key. Three difficulty tiers — from routine traffic to timed flash missions where one wrong letter ends the transmission.

Code Breaker

Intercept

An enemy transmission comes through — a single 5-letter word in Morse. Listen carefully, decode what you hear, and crack the word in 5 attempts or fewer.

Explosive Ordnance

Bomb Disposal

Decode Morse clues to defuse a multi-stage bomb before time runs out. Cut wires, press buttons, flip switches — each instruction arrives as a coded signal. Wrong move costs you precious seconds.

Signal Overflow

Survive

Incoming messages pile up on a typewriter faster than you can decode them. Earn power-ups to stay alive, but the pace never stops accelerating. How long can you hold the line before the signal overflows?
