Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 14, 2026

Morse Operator is built with your privacy in mind. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data Collection

Morse Operator does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. The app operates entirely offline.

Local Data

The app stores your game progress, statistics, and settings locally on your device using on-device storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Analytics & Tracking

Morse Operator does not use any analytics services, advertising frameworks, or tracking tools. No usage data is collected.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

Morse Operator does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at privacy@morseoperator.com.
